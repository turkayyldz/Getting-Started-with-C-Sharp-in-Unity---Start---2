Title:				Liberty Leading The PeopleLiberty Leading The People

Author:				Eugène Delacroix

Description:			Liberty Leading the People is a painting by Eugène Delacroix commemorating the July Revolution of 1830, which toppled King Charles X of France. 
				A woman of the people with a Phrygian cap personifying the concept of Liberty leads a varied group of people forward over a barricade and the bodies of the fallen, 
				holding the flag of the French Revolution in one hand and brandishing a bayonetted musket with the other.

Location:			Louvre, Paris
